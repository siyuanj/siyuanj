## Hi there

I am **Siyuan Jiang**, an undergraduate student at **Tsinghua University, Tanwei College**, majoring in **Chemical Biology for Pharmaceutical Science**.

My work sits around computational protein engineering: protein design models, virtual screening pipelines, evolutionary analysis, and practical tools that make structural biology workflows less painful.

## Current Focus

- Designing efficient screening pipelines for functional food-derived peptides.
- Studying the spatiotemporal evolution of the gamma-secretase complex.
- Building research utilities for AlphaFold 3 batch submission, Zotero workflows, and bioinformatics analysis.
- Learning better ways to connect model-driven predictions with experimental validation.
